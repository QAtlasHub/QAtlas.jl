TFIM — model index

The 1D transverse-field Ising model — the canonical exactly-solvable quantum phase transition, Jordan-Wigner dual to free fermions and critical at $h = J$.

\[H = -J\sum_i \sigma^z_i \sigma^z_{i+1} - h\sum_i \sigma^x_i\]
